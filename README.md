# Stem-racing-car-development
This documentation shows the process of the design development and aerodynamic optimization of our stem racing car. The design focused of minimizing drag and making sure that the pressure is well distributed on the car by testing it varies times in ansys and on fusion.

## Development Workflow
A dedicated workflow summary has been added to document body, wing, and wheel development in one place.
